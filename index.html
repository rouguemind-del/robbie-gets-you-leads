Commercial Cleaning and Janitorial Service Palm Beach County

Since 1979, Crystal Building Maintenance has provided professional commercial cleaning to its customers. Our team has been providing commercial cleaning services and janitorial services for over 45 years in Palm Beach County. Crystal Building Maintenance services buildings, offices, banks, medical facilities, municipalities, country clubs, condo/HOA associations, schools, restaurants, theaters, industrial businesses, retail stores and all large and small businesses.

Time Study Pricing Methodology

While many companies base their fees on the square footage of buildings or office space, our fees are based upon a developed quoting program using a time study analysis. This method takes into account the amount of time and manpower needed to successfully complete our work. Because of this, we are able to provide top quality work at a very cost efficient rate.

Experienced and Trained Team

As a trusted professional service provider in the cleaning industry, Crystal Building Maintenance provides constant training to our experienced staff to better utilize the advanced cleaning equipment, tools and chemicals made today. We also offer environmentally green cleaning chemicals and techniques if requested.

Quality Assurance Commitment

Crystal Building Maintenance provides personal customer care contact to ensure your satisfaction with our work. An experienced supervisor oversees the job site following a daily work schedule. A Customer Relations/Quality Assurance Inspector visits each job site to meet with the facility manager during office hours to ensure that expectations are met or exceeded as well as to address and correct any issues. We devote a substantial amount of time and money to train our staff to provide top quality cleaning that is second to none!

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond for emergency cleaning?

We offer 24/7 emergency service across Palm Beach County. For urgent cleaning like flood damage or facility sanitization, we typically respond within 2-4 hours. Same-day service is available for most emergency situations.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all commercial cleaning projects. We'll assess your facility, discuss options, and provide a detailed written estimate at no cost.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed and insured with comprehensive coverage. We've been serving Palm Beach County since 1979, ensuring professional craftsmanship on every project.

What areas do you serve in Palm Beach County?

We serve all of Palm Beach County including West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, Jupiter, Wellington, Lake Worth, Palm Beach Gardens, and surrounding areas.

Do you offer green cleaning options?

Yes, we offer environmentally friendly cleaning chemicals and techniques upon request. Our green cleaning methods are safe for employees while maintaining the highest cleaning standards.

Do you clean up after the job is complete?

Absolutely. We believe in leaving your property cleaner than we found it. All equipment is properly stored, surfaces are restored, and we do a thorough cleanup before considering any job complete.
