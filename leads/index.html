Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lead lists

How fresh are the leads?

Our leads are pulled fresh weekly from multiple data sources. Expired listings are typically 30-90 days old, FSBOs are current active listings, and pre-foreclosure/probate leads are updated monthly from court records.

What information is included with each lead?

Every lead includes: property owner name, property address, phone number (skip-traced), email address (when available), property details, and lead source type. We also include last known good contact date when possible.

Do you scrub against the Do Not Call list?

Yes, all our monthly subscription plans include automatic DNC scrubbing. For one-time lists, we can add DNC scrubbing for an additional $5 per list upon request.

What markets do you cover?

We currently serve DFW, Houston, Phoenix, Atlanta, San Antonio, and Tampa markets. We're expanding to new markets monthly based on demand. Contact us if you need a different market.

Can I request specific zip codes or neighborhoods?

Absolutely! For Pro lists and all subscription plans, we can filter by specific zip codes, price ranges, property types, or neighborhoods. Just include your preferences in your order.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the quality or accuracy of your leads, we'll refund your purchase or provide replacement leads at no charge.
