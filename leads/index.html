Lead Types We Provide

Targeted seller leads for every prospecting strategy

Expired Listings

Homeowners whose listings expired without selling. Motivated, often frustrated, and ready for a new agent who can deliver results.

FSBO

For-sale-by-owner sellers who often discover they need professional help. High conversion potential for agents who offer value first.

Pre-Foreclosure

Homeowners facing foreclosure who need to sell quickly. Time-sensitive situations where a fast, empathetic approach wins listings.

Probate

Properties inherited through probate proceedings. Heirs often want to sell quickly and prefer working with a knowledgeable agent.

Vacant Properties

Unoccupied properties where owners may be open to selling. Often off-market opportunities that other agents miss entirely.
