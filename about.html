Since 1979: A Legacy of Excellence

For over 45 years, Crystal Building Maintenance has been the trusted choice for professional commercial cleaning services throughout Palm Beach County, Florida. What started as a small local business has grown into one of the region's most respected commercial cleaning companies.

Our longevity in this industry speaks to our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We've weathered economic changes, industry evolution, and countless challenges by maintaining our core values: superior service, fair pricing, and genuine care for our clients' success.

Our Professional Approach

Time Study Pricing

Unlike many companies that base their fees solely on square footage, we use a comprehensive quoting program that utilizes time study analysis. This method considers the actual time and manpower needed to complete the work successfully, ensuring you get accurate, fair pricing for top-quality service.

Experienced & Trained Staff

Our team receives constant training on the latest cleaning equipment, tools, and chemicals. We invest substantially in our staff's education to ensure they can utilize advanced cleaning technology effectively and safely. Environmental green cleaning techniques are available upon request.

Quality Assurance Program

Every job site has an experienced supervisor who follows a detailed daily work schedule. Additionally, our Customer Relations/Quality Assurance Inspector visits each location during business hours to meet with facility managers, ensuring expectations are not just met, but exceeded.

Personal Customer Care

We provide direct customer care contact to ensure your complete satisfaction with our work. Our commitment to communication means issues are addressed quickly and thoroughly, maintaining the high standards that have kept our clients with us for decades.
