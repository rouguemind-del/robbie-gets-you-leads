Advanced Disinfection and Technical Commercial Cleaning Services

We leverage the latest innovations to stay ahead of pathogens and allergens. Our technical commercial cleaning services include specialized treatments that many standard vendors cannot provide:

1. Electrostatic Disinfection

We use positively charged particles to wrap around surfaces, providing 360-degree coverage. This is essential for preventing the spread of flu, COVID-19, and other communicable diseases in shared environments.

2. High-Performance Floor Care

From stripping and waxing VCT to deep-cleaning grout lines in restrooms, our floor care program extends the life of your assets while maintaining a high coefficient of friction to reduce slip-and-fall liability.

3. Green Cleaning Initiatives

Upon request, we utilize non-toxic, biodegradable products that support indoor air quality. This "Green Cleaning" approach is ideal for eco-conscious corporate offices looking to reduce their environmental footprint.
