What We Do

Our Services

From safe bee removal to agricultural consulting, we offer comprehensive services to protect both your property and our precious pollinators.

Live Bee Removal

Safe, humane removal of honey bee colonies from homes, businesses, and structures. We never exterminate -- every colony is carefully preserved and relocated.

Bee Relocation

Ethical relocation of bee colonies to local apiaries where they can thrive. Every bee we remove gets a new home and continues its vital role in pollination.

Agricultural Exemptions

We help property owners qualify for valuable agricultural tax exemptions through managed beehive placement. Save on taxes while supporting local ecosystems.

Education & Workshops

Community-focused beekeeping education, workshops, and presentations. Learn about the vital role bees play and how to coexist with these incredible pollinators.

Local Honey Sales

Award-winning pure Florida honey harvested right here in the Treasure Coast. Our honey earned a blue ribbon at the Florida State Honey Show.

Hobe Sound Honeybees LLC is a family-owned business built on a deep love for honey bees and a commitment to their preservation. We believe every colony deserves a chance to thrive, which is why we specialize in live removal -- never extermination.
