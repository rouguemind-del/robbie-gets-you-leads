About Long Island Wall Pro

30+ Years of Professional Experience

Long Island Wall Pro was established in 2017, but our expertise spans over three decades. Our owner is a skilled craftsman trained through the prestigious Local 1486 Painter's/Taper's Union, bringing union-level quality and professionalism to every project.

This combination of extensive professional experience and union training ensures that every job meets the highest industry standards. From complex commercial projects to elegant residential transformations, we have the skills and knowledge to handle any challenge.

We believe that every property deserves the same level of attention and quality finish you'd find in the most exclusive Hampton estates. That's why our slogan reflects our mission: "You don't have to live in the Hamptons for your house to look like it does!"
