About

Hello, I'm Ivette

I'm a South Florida photographer based in Palm Beach County, specializing in capturing life's most beautiful moments with a warm, natural aesthetic. From the golden glow of a sunset engagement session to the tender joy of a growing family, I believe every story deserves to be told with intention and artistry.

My approach is relaxed, genuine, and full of warmth. I want you to feel completely at ease so that your truest, most joyful selves shine through in every frame. Whether we're on the beach, in a sun-drenched field, or exploring the charming streets of South Florida, I'll guide you through every moment.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me soaking up the Florida sunshine with my own family -- which is probably why I love what I do so much.

- Ivette
