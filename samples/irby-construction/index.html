A Legacy of Excellence in Power Construction

Irby Construction Company began as a family business in Richland, Mississippi, and has carried that close-knit culture forward through decades of growth and transformation. Today, as a proud subsidiary of Quanta Services, Inc. -- the largest specialty contractor in North America -- we combine the resources of a Fortune 500 organization with the values of a family-owned company.

Our extraordinary record of success spans high-voltage transmission, renewable energy, distribution, telecommunications, and beyond. With nationwide reach and international project experience, Irby Construction has earned the trust of utilities, municipalities, and private partners across the country.

We don't just build infrastructure. We build relationships, careers, and communities -- one project at a time.

What We Do
Comprehensive Power Infrastructure Services

Delivering end-to-end solutions for America's energy needs, from high-voltage transmission to emergency storm restoration.

Transmission

Design and construction of high-voltage power line systems, lattice towers, and steel pole structures across long-distance corridors.

Renewables

Building the infrastructure that connects renewable energy sources to the grid, including solar, wind, and battery storage interconnections.

Distribution

Power distribution system construction and maintenance, ensuring reliable electricity delivery to homes and businesses.

Telecommunications

Installation and construction of telecommunications infrastructure including fiber optic networks and communication towers.

Substation

Complete substation construction, expansion, and connection services to support power generation and distribution networks.

Outdoor Lighting

Commercial and municipal lighting design and installation for highways, sports facilities, parking areas, and public spaces.

Foundations

Engineered foundation systems for transmission structures, substations, and power infrastructure requiring specialized structural support.

Emergency Restoration

Rapid-response storm damage restoration and emergency power system repair, available around the clock when disaster strikes.
